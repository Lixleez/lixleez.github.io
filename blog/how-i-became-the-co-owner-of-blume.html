How I Became The Co-Owner Of Blume

One day, I was snooping around a page called CUG, now commonly known as Nooble. That's when I found the contact info for the owner, and we came up with an idea to start Blume. At that time, I started dating thedevmarcus (a dev of Nooble), and together we created a few projects, including the Gen Z Translator.
